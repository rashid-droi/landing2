About SELECT

Specialists in UAE student success

SELECT Training and Management Consultancy LLC is a UAE-based organisation supporting UAE students studying abroad. With a deep understanding of UAE culture and national priorities, we partner with sponsors, families, and students throughout the journey.

Our Arabic-speaking counsellors and academic advisors combine expertise in education management, psychometric assessment, cultural intelligence, and workforce readiness.

Unsupported students face severe risks

UAE government scholarships provide immense value, yet nearly half of self-funded Emirati students are clustered in just two universities. The risk of unguided choices, academic underperformance, and emotional burnout is deeply prominent without structured care.
